MISSION

--------

A non-partisan immigrant-led initiative
to inform people about minority voter suppression and increase participation.

Message from the Founder

---------------

Immigrants are the bedrock of the American economy. Ironically, plenty of states have passed voter suppression laws to disenfranchise these voters. Through these laws, they have been successful in preventing millions of minority voters from exercising their right to vote and changing the outcome of the US elections.

Not anymore.

As immigrants, we can no longer be spectators on the sidelines. It's time we took the stage front and center. It's time we used our power to amplify voices that need to be heard and uplift those wrongfully overshadowed. We may be the minority, but our contribution to this country and our influence knows no bounds.

In the eyes of the constitution, all people are created equal. It's time we made good on that promise.

Sincerely,
Deep Jethmalani
